HISTORY

SINCE 1982


Since 1982, Canterbury Gardens and Gifts has offered their customers beautiful gifts for all seasons from their Escondido store situated in Southern California on the site of an old winery with lovely rose gardens and an inviting gazebo. Owners, Dave and Al, have fashioned the store into a visual feast for the senses. Canterbury Gardens and Gifts is renowned for its large selection of quality merchandise. Our well stocked warehouse enables us to provide the items you want, exactly when you want them! Whether you purchase online or visit us in Escondido, our large inventory insures that your selected items are either ready for shipping or ready to take home.
