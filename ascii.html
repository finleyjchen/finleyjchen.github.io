The game design is going under production right now.

It will be a 1000x1000 character board. The board refreshes to redraw the scene.

Using hand drawn pictures, they will be converted into ascii art. Animation will happen with simple hand drawn spritesheets.

So far I am thinking to make it a randomly generated platformer, with different classes of weapons and several bosses. Boss design will be fun. I will take insight from many sources, maybe put up a recommendation box. This website will document my development.
